What is PowerPoint Karaoke?

Ever wanted to hang out with friends but wanted to do it in a unique way? Why not check out some presentations made by members of the online commnity! By browsing out expansive library, you’ll be sure to find something that peaks you and your friends interest. You can even upload your own presentations to the site and educate other people on whatever topic you want to document about. This is just another way to get together with others when being in person doesn’t work but you still want to have fun.
